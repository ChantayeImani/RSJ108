How To Make A Thick Tweet

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR108

How to make your tweet thicker than a snicker

Assembly time: 10 minutes
Awesome rating: 100000000000000000000% awesome

How do you tweet?

You should alway's start by asking yourself questions.

  • Things You'll Need to complete a thick tweet

  • A brain
  • Creative thoughts
  • Resources
  • Time

  1. Having a message

    Ask yourself What is your message?Is your message clear? Is the message concise? If you don’t have a clear message it will be hard for the consumer to understand the point of your tweet.

  2. Delivery

    If you're going to deliver your message, what is the tone and mood? Does it match what is being presented?Do you have a strong delivery of the message?

    Punctuation is key

    With your Message and delivery are you wording things clear in AP style? As a journalist the way you use punctuation will help with credibility and style wise will be good for a professional look.

  3. Picking the best media for your tweet

    Since we now know how the message will be delivered we want to deliver a thick tweet to our audience. Now it's time to think about what we are going to pick to go along with the tweet? Are you going to pick an image,video, or a gif?

    What does your tweet call for?How will it best represent your style and vision?

    • Image

    • Video

    • Gif

  4. Attribution

    Remember with a thick tweet to include hashtags, @’s, locations,website short links and more to give people credit where it is needed and to source information. This helps with giving credit to the original owner as well as providing sources for viewers to go to the right outlet.

  5. An Unsuccessful Thick Tweet

    Reasons why this tweet is unsuccessful

      • The tweet is opinionated and not backed up by facts.

      • No hashtags or depth.

      • The tweet isn’t professional

    If you’re able to spot the rest you are already on track!

  6. Conclusion

    In Conclusion a successful tweet is a thick tweet. A thick tweet has information worderd in a way that gets the viewer’s attention and get’s the main point across . A thick tweet has 5 sentences max or less . It also has proper grammar and punctuation . In a successful thick tweet you pay attribution this helps give credit to whowever previously posted the content you are referencing . This includes names of pictures used, locations of places visited, names of brands and more. Remember to be credible and don’t plagiarize.
